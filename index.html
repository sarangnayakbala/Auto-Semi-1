♡ Letter for You ♡

Will you be my Valentine?

MAAO

From the first time I saw you, I felt something shift.
Not dramatic. Not loud. Just different.
There’s something about you the way you smile without trying, the way you stay calm when I overthink, even the little chaos you carry it all feels real in a way I can’t explain.


The moments we share don’t just pass by. They stay.
I replay them without meaning to. And honestly, I don’t want you to become just another beautiful memory.
I want something that lasts.
